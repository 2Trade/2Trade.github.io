How to Create a Logo - 10 step tutorial

1. Learn logo basics.
A good logo must be simple, memorable, enduring, versatile, and finally, appropriate.
2. Sketch concepts.
Be original! Keep your design simple, and avoid things like clipart and generic shapes. If your logo has more than 3 colors, you're doing it wrong.
3. Analyze your best sketches.
Choose the top 3 designs and share them with other people. Absorb feedback and use them to finalize the idea.
4. Configure logo layout.
Make sure everything is spaced out equally. Remember that it doesn't have to be symmetrical, only spacing matters.
5. Select flexible color options.
Sometimes logos don't "work" on certain backgrounds. Be sure to have color choices for light and dark backgrounds.
6. Choose a good, memorable font.
Choose a good font that defines your company. For example, serif fonts symbolize "old" while sans serif fonts symbolize "new." Be sure to avoid a generic font like Times New Roman or Comic Sans.
7. Maintain font flexibility.
Your logo needs to be able to be scaled into any size, large enough for billboards and small enough for business cards. Remember that the logo needs to be legible in any size.
